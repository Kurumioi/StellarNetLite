Quick examples

Default KCP test:

powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File .\Assets\StreamingAssets\StellarNetLiteLoadTest\run-loadtest.ps1

5 rooms, 20 clients per room, KCP, run until stopped:

powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File .\Assets\StreamingAssets\StellarNetLiteLoadTest\run-loadtest.ps1 -Transport kcp -ServerHost 127.0.0.1 -Port 7777 -Rooms 5 -ClientsPerRoom 20 -Duration 0 -MoveRate 8

10 rooms, 30 clients per room, slower connect rate:

powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File .\Assets\StreamingAssets\StellarNetLiteLoadTest\run-loadtest.ps1 -Transport kcp -ServerHost 127.0.0.1 -Port 7777 -Rooms 10 -ClientsPerRoom 30 -ConnectRate 20 -Duration 0 -MoveRate 8

TCP comparison:

powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File .\Assets\StreamingAssets\StellarNetLiteLoadTest\run-loadtest.ps1 -Transport tcp -ServerHost 127.0.0.1 -Port 7777 -Rooms 5 -ClientsPerRoom 20 -Duration 0 -MoveRate 8
